Liffey Barber

Liffey Barber has served the Community of Newbridge for over a decade. Since opening our doors in 2010, we see ourselves as a modern barbers with an old-school twist. Our services are suitable for all ages, from your grandfather to his grandchild. We pride ourselves on customer service and excellent hair too. Liffey Barber boast of being the best in town. We look forward to welcoming you to our shop.

Edel

From owning her own business for over eleven years to managing barbershops throughout her career, spanning three decades, Edel is one of the most experienced barbers in the industry. She prides herself on customer service.

Joey

Joey is our senior barber who has been a valuable part of our team since 2017. A big personality and an expert with the clippers. For fades or the more classic cut, Joey has got you covered.

Amber

Amber is the newest member of our team and she is proving to be a natural talent. In an industry normally dominated by the boys, we are proud to watch her go from strength to strength.
